Classes and Rates

I teach my students one to one in the students' preferred location. Most of my students ask that I go to their house, while others feel more comfortable in a public setting such as a cafe. I live around the Tokyo Bay area and am willing to travel around Central Tokyo, but require my transportation to be covered separately from the cost of the lessons.

I typically charge around 3,000 yen an hour excluding the transportation fee. For group classes starting from three students, the price per student decreases. Group classes may not go over six students.
